About

James has a deep knowledge in history, and combat skills, he's looking for a missing treasure in a big castle. He was requested by his mentor to find this big fortune. He faces alot of troubles in his adventure. As we know, enemies are everywhere, there are two people who are trying to get the treasure from James and his friend.

Story

David found the map and give to James so he can know the path he's going through, and asked him to go and steal it, regarding the skills he get in fighting and historic experience.

Start the journey trying to find the treasure, and get into challenging and deadly situations. Try to get the treasure from the enemies who's looking for the same fortune too.

After a challenging experience and plenty of fights, and after solving the hardest puzzle in the history, they have reached the treasure and get their fortune.
